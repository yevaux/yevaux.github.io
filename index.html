I'm Yeva,


An experienced B2C and B2B UX researcher with a background in analytics.

Currently, I lead mid-funnel research initiatives at Groupon focusing on core consumer experiences: deal pages, pricing, cartability and checkout, bookings, bundling, repeat purchases, and customer trust.

Previously, I was a part of the Google Chrome OS team where I conducted multi-phase foundational and tactical research with enterprise and end users. My focus areas included advanced security, onboarding, and knowledge worker experience. Before that, I worked at Microsoft Azure on the Customer Experience Platform team under the Cloud & AI Design Studio, where I performed a range of studies to improve user experience within the existing product ecosystem and evaluate future-facing designs.

Before transitioning to UX, I managed global business analytics for Google Cloud's Apps and Maps for Business renewals and researched ways to optimize internal data management systems.

I hold a master's degree in Human-Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE), a top-ranked UX program at the University of Washington. My undergraduate degree is from the Haas Business School at UC Berkeley.
